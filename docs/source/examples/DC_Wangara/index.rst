Diurnal Cycle: Wangara
======================

The Wangara case simulates a full diurnal cycle from 0900 LST 16 August 1967
to 0900 LST 17 August 1967 (Day 33–34 of the 1967 Wangara experiment).
The reference domain is 5000 m × 5000 m × 2000 m on an 80\ :sup:`3` grid
(Basu et al. 2008, *Boundary-Layer Meteorology*, 129, 329-359).

The surface boundary condition uses observed screen-level potential temperature
at 1.2 m above the ground, and the geostrophic wind is time- and height-varying.
