Stable BL: GABLS1
=================

The GABLS1 case simulates a stably stratified nocturnal boundary layer
driven by a prescribed surface cooling rate of 0.25 K hr\ :sup:`-1`
over a 9-hour period (Beare et al. 2006, *Boundary-Layer Meteorology*,
118, 247-272). The reference domain is 400 m x 400 m x 400 m.

For a step-by-step walkthrough of setting up, running, and
post-processing the packaged 100\ :sup:`3` LASDD-SM run, see the :doc:`Tutorial
</tutorial/Tutorial_notebook>`.
