Stable BL: GABLS3
=================

The GABLS3 case simulates a stably stratified nocturnal boundary layer
over Cabauw, Netherlands (51.97°N, 4.93°E) during the night of 1–2 July 2006
(00–09 UTC). The reference domain is 800 m × 800 m × 800 m on a 128\ :sup:`3`
grid. Unlike GABLS1, this case includes time- and height-varying geostrophic
winds, large-scale temperature and moisture advection, and a prognostic specific
humidity field driven by observed 0.25 m surface temperature and humidity
(Bosveld et al. 2020, *Journal of the Atmospheric Sciences*, 77, 3343–3362).
