\caption{Schematic workflow of the JAX-ALFA solver. Run-specific configuration is loaded before initialization, followed by a time-marching loop that applies filtering, surface fluxes, gradients, advection, buoyancy, subgrid-scale closure, pressure projection, and Adams-Bashforth time advancement. The SGS pathway supports four dynamic variants: LASDD-SM, LASDD-WL, LAD-SM, and LAD-WL. At prescribed dynamic-update instants, three-dimensional SGS coefficients are recomputed; on intervening steps, the most recently computed coefficients are reused by the stress and scalar-flux routines.}
\label{fig:jaxalfa-codebase-flowchart}
